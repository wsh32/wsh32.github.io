Systems Engineer

Our team, Helix Industries, competed in the 2016 MATE ROV Competition. Our task involved using a Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) to complete predetermined tasks. These tasks included collecting temperature readings, identifying equipment, obtaining oil samples and analysing their gas chromatographs, taking photographs of coral colonies, and attaching a flange and wellhead cap to the top of a decommissioned wellhead. In order to complete these, we made a ROV specialized in performing the tasks.
